Stranded. Yes, she was now the first person on Venus.

Ever to land here, but that fact meant little now.

Her name would be read by millions in school someday.

As the first to land here, but never alive again.

She looked at the broken control panel and felt despair.

Nothing would ever get it back into working order again.

She was the first, and it was tragically not clear.

This would also be her last thought as hope faded.
